1. What Chess Journal is

Chess Journal is proprietary, closed-source desktop and Android software for recording, analyzing, and studying chess games.

2. The free tier

Chess Journal is free to download and use indefinitely for: playing and recording your own games, importing from Chess.com and Lichess, browsing your own game library, the board editor, themes and autoplay, live opening detection, and the "Eco" Stockfish preset. No account, payment, or time limit applies to these features.

3. The $5 unlock

A one-time, non-subscription payment of $5 unlocks the remaining features described on the Pricing page (deeper Stockfish presets, on-device AI commentary, the Classics/Puzzles/Theory libraries, the Assistive Coach, PDF export, and the stats dashboards) for the lifetime of the application, including future updates to those features.

  • On Windows and Linux, the unlock is activated with a license key tied to your purchase, verified entirely offline on your device.
  • On Android, the unlock is purchased and verified through Google Play Billing, subject to Google Play's own terms of sale in addition to these terms.
